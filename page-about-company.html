Our Story

— Story Behind The Royal Splits

For years, ThemeWagon templates have stood for beautiful design and excellent quality. Posh is more than all that; it's built to take on the future - with rethink coding structures, great feel and ideal balance of performance & elegance. What's the best, Posh will stay error free & up to date with the excellent customer support and regular updates.

— Mission & Vision

Posh has the vision to be the trend setter among the existing templates in the market. It intends to help people do more and leave lasting, meaningful experiences for their clients and their audiences. The mission was to make it real. The core team worked very hard for last three years to make it happen. They believed in their abilities and created Posh as the right tool for your future.
